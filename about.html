Project Introduction

Hidden Verdicts is a digital crime awareness and reporting platform designed to bring hidden incidents into the light. It allows individuals to anonymously report crimes, explore historical events, and view real community experiences. The platform aims to create a safer society by encouraging transparency and awareness.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower individuals to speak up against crime without fear.

Vision: To build a community-driven platform where awareness leads to prevention and justice.

Key Features

📍 Timeline

Explore major crime events across Mumbai in an interactive visual format.

📝 Reporting

Submit anonymous crime reports securely with details and evidence.

🌍 Public Feed

View real stories shared by individuals to raise awareness.

Why This Project Matters

Many crimes go unreported due to fear, stigma, or lack of trust in systems. Hidden Verdicts bridges this gap by providing a safe and anonymous environment for individuals to share their experiences, ultimately contributing to awareness, prevention, and justice.
