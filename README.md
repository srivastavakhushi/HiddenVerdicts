# Hidden Verdicts

What happens when four friends who love reading about and solving criminal cases decide to do a project?

Well, Hidden Verdicts is the answer.

Developed by four crime enthusiasts-cum-college students, Hidden Verdicts is a full-fledged crime website based on the crimes of India's financial capital—Bombay.

Hidden Verdicts was more of a passion project than a classroom one. It was developed by people who had to search through a million websites just to learn more about a single crime. It's made for all the people who are interested in knowing more about crimes but don't really know where to start.

The idea behind Hidden Verdicts was simple-to create a single platform where users can learn about crimes, stay updated with recent incidents, access official resources, and even contribute towards spreading awareness. Instead of jumping from one website to another, users can find everything they need in one place.

Not only is Hidden Verdicts user-friendly, but it also includes a lot of other interesting features.

Features that distinguish Hidden Verdicts from an ordinary crime website are:

1. The All-in-One Solution

Whether it be reading about crimes or reporting them, everything is available on our site. Hidden Verdicts combines awareness, information, and action on a single platform.

2. Home Page

The Home Page features all the major areas of Mumbai, and the same grid can redirect users to two different links—the official police website and the most recent news from that area. This helps users stay informed while also providing direct access to official resources.

3. The Form

The reporting form allows users to report crimes and even uses Google Maps to help pinpoint the location. It also includes a public feed where users can view different crimes that have happened to people, thereby increasing awareness and encouraging community participation.

4. Timeline

The most interesting feature of our website—the Timeline. It consists of a movable scroller and an interactive timeline of major Bombay crimes, along with information about each incident. The timeline allows users to explore the city's criminal history in a more engaging and visual manner.

What Makes Hidden Verdicts Different?

Unlike traditional crime websites that focus only on news articles, Hidden Verdicts combines crime awareness, crime reporting, historical crime documentation, and public engagement within a single platform. By bringing together the past and present, the website helps users understand how crime has evolved over time while also keeping them informed about current incidents.

Our Goal

Through Hidden Verdicts, we hope to create a community that is more aware, informed, and responsible. Our goal is not only to educate people about crimes but also to encourage them to speak up, stay vigilant, and contribute towards building a safer society.

After all, every verdict has a story—and sometimes, the most important ones are the ones hidden from plain sight.
